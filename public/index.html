I am a first-year PhD student in Electrical, Computer, and Energy Engineering at Arizona State University, advised by Prof. Shaofeng Zou.

My research focuses on reinforcement learning, large language models, and their applications to healthcare, with particular interest in safe and efficient reinforcement learning and constrained decision-making.

Previously, I received my B.Eng. in Automation from Xi’an Jiaotong University.
